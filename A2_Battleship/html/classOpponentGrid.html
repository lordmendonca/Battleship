void OpponentGrid::shotResult ( const Shot &  shot,
Shot::Impact  impact 
)

Method that updates the shots on the opponent grid using the shot received and the type of impact associated with the shot.

Parameters
[in]shot: grid position that was shot by the player.
[in]impact: impact at that grid position, enumerated as Shot::NONE, Shot::HIT or Shot::SUNKEN, and provided (orally) by the opponent player.

This method also updates the vector container of sunken ships when impact received is Shot::SUNKEN.

◆ shots

std::map<GridPosition,Shot::Impact> OpponentGrid::shots
private

A member attribute and a container of type map that stores the grid position which was shot by the player against the opponent player and its consequent impact as informed by the opponent, as a pair.

Key of the map is the grid position of the shot that the player made.

Value of the map for the corresponding key is the impact at that grid position as informed by the opponent, enumerated as Shot::NONE, Shot::HIT or Shot::SUNKEN.
