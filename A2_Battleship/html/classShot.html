Member Enumeration Documentation

◆ Impact

enum Shot::Impact

Enumerator that associates with the shot as either hit the ship, sunk the ship or missed the ship.

NONE : The shot has no effect (missed).

HIT : The shot has hit one of the occupied area (grid position) of a ship.

SUNKEN : The shot has sunk the whole ship.
